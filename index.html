FAQ & Information

About This App

The CxE Americas Offsite 2025 Step Tracker is a fun, interactive application designed to encourage physical activity and team engagement during our offsite event. Participants can track their daily steps, compete with colleagues, and complete location-based challenges.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+)
  • Storage: Browser Local Storage
  • Icons: Font Awesome
  • Fonts: Google Fonts (Inter)
  • Weather: Mock weather service (demo purposes)

Data Storage & Privacy

Local Storage Only: All your data (steps, goals, team info) is stored locally in your browser using Local Storage. No data is sent to external servers.

Data Removal: Your data will be automatically removed when you clear your browser's data or after the browser's storage quota management. You can also manually reset your data from the Profile section.

Privacy: Since data is stored locally, only you have access to your step information on your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate is the step tracking?

A: This app requires manual step entry. For best results, use a fitness tracker or phone app to count steps, then enter them here.

Q: Can I see other people's steps?

A: You can see aggregated leaderboard data and team statistics, but individual detailed step data is private to each user.

Q: What happens to my data after the offsite?

A: Data remains on your device until you clear your browser data. The app will continue to work normally after the event.

Q: I lost my data, can it be recovered?

A: Unfortunately, since data is stored locally, it cannot be recovered if browser data is cleared. Consider taking screenshots of your achievements!
