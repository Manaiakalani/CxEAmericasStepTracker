FAQ & Information

Privacy & Data Usage Notice

Your Privacy Matters: Any information you provide (name/username and step data) is used exclusively for the purpose of team morale and engagement during the CxE Americas Offsite 2025 event.

Data Usage: Your name/username and steps are only identified and used for the step tracking game throughout the week. This data is stored securely and will be permanently deleted less than 14 days after the offsite is completed.

Minimal Data: We only collect what's necessary - your chosen display name and step counts. No personal information, location data, or device information is stored.

About This App

The CxE Americas Offsite 2025 Step Tracker is a comprehensive, interactive application designed to encourage physical activity and team engagement during our offsite event. Features include:

  • Personal Dashboard: Track daily steps, view progress, and monitor achievements
  • Team Competition: Join teams and compete with colleagues
  • Live Leaderboards: Real-time individual and team rankings
  • Activity Feed: See recent activities with colorful icons
  • Live Display: Large-screen display perfect for presentations and events
  • Cloud Sync: Automatic data synchronization across devices
  • Mobile Optimized: Works seamlessly on phones, tablets, and desktops
  • Admin Tools: Administrative dashboard for event management

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+) with responsive design
  • Database: Supabase PostgreSQL with real-time synchronization
  • Storage: Cloud-first architecture with localStorage fallback
  • Icons: Font Awesome with color-coded activity types
  • Fonts: Google Fonts (Inter) for modern typography
  • Real-time Features: Auto-refresh, live activity feeds, connection monitoring
  • PWA Ready: Service worker for offline capabilities

Data Storage & Sync

Cloud-First Architecture: Your data is primarily stored in a secure cloud database (Supabase) for reliable access across devices and real-time synchronization with teammates.

Smart Fallback: If cloud connection is unavailable, data is stored locally in your browser and automatically synced when connection is restored.

Real-time Sync: Changes are instantly shared across all your devices and reflected in team leaderboards and activity feeds.

Connection Monitoring: The app automatically monitors your connection and switches between cloud and local storage as needed.

Data Security: All data is encrypted in transit and stored securely. Only your display name and step counts are stored - no personal information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate is the step tracking?

A: This app requires manual step entry from your fitness tracker, phone app, or smartwatch.

Q: Can I see other people's steps?

A: You can see everyone's steps in the leaderboards and team statistics - this creates friendly competition and team engagement! Individual activity details are visible in the activity feeds with colorful icons.

Q: What happens to my data after the offsite?

A: All data will be permanently deleted less than 14 days after the offsite ends. You can also manually clear your data anytime from the Profile section.

Q: I lost my data, can it be recovered?

A: With cloud sync enabled (default), your data is automatically backed up and synced across devices. If you clear browser data, simply reload the page and your cloud data will be restored.

Q: How often does the data refresh?

A: The app uses real-time synchronization! New activities appear immediately when added, leaderboards auto-refresh every 5 minutes, and the Live Display updates every 30 seconds.

Q: Does this work on mobile devices?

A: Absolutely! The app is fully responsive with touch-friendly controls, mobile-optimized layouts, and works great on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Q: What is the Live Display feature?

A: The Live Display is perfect for projecting during events. It shows real-time leaderboards, team stats, and activity feeds with auto-refresh and connection monitoring.

Q: What are the colorful icons in activities?

A: Activities now have color-coded FontAwesome icons! Green for steps, gold for achievements, cyan for new users, purple for team changes, orange for overachievers, and more.

Q: Can administrators manage the system?

A: Yes! The Admin Dashboard provides comprehensive management tools, user oversight, manual step entry, system diagnostics, and database testing to ensure the app is running great throughout the week. Have an issue, let Max Stein or one of our great CxE admins know.

Important Information

Event Purpose: This app is designed exclusively for the CxE Americas Offsite 2025 event to boost team morale and encourage physical activity during the week.

Data Lifecycle: All participant data will be permanently and securely deleted less than 14 days after the offsite concludes. No data will be retained beyond this period.
